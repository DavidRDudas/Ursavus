How we hire.

Rarely, slowly, and for the long term.

We would rather leave a seat empty than fill it carelessly. There is no application portal — send us a note and something you have made: a game, a jam entry, a repository, a write-up. We play it and read it ourselves.

We value taste and depth over breadth of buzzwords, and we expect everyone here to be able to carry an idea from a rough sketch to a playable prototype to a shipped game. If that is you, the empty seat might be yours.
